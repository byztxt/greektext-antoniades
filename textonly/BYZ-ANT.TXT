The following collation comparing the Robinson-Pierpont Byzantine
Textform 2005 edition (hereafter Byz05) and the Antoniades 1904/1912
Patriarchal edition (hereafter Antoniades) was prepared electronically,
with complete checking and verification by Maurice A. Robinson and
Jussi Ala-Konni.

Unless otherwise specified, the Antoniades 1904 edition agrees with its
corrected 1912 reprint. Entries marked as "(sic)" indicate errors,
typographical or syntactical, as well as otherwise questionable
readings that appear in one or both of the Antoniades editions.

Note that this collation does not indicate words appearing in small
type in the Antoniades 1904/1912 editions whenever such read
identically with Byz05. The complete listing of all small type words
(bracketed [ ] in the present edition) that appear within the
Antoniades 1904/1912 editions is provided in the separate file
ANTSMALL.PRN.

COLLATION FORMAT:

BYZ05 ] ANTONIADES

Lu 17:36  Byz05 contains no text.
Ac 8:37   Byz05 contains no text.
Ac 15:34  Byz05 contains no text.
Ac 24:7   Byz05 contains no text.

There are 1555 differences between Byz05 and the Antoniades 1912
corrected edition. Due to various errors or alternate variants, the
Antoniades 1904 edition contains 6 erroneous readings, as well as
agreement with Byz05 in 2 instances; thus there exists a net difference
of 4, giving a total of 1559 differences for the Antoniades 1904
edition.
